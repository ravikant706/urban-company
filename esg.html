Our ESG Priorities

We have identified five priority goals that align well with our vision, mission and core platform operations. These goals will help us contribute to building a better world for everyone.

Good health and well-being

Ensure healthy lives & promote well-being for all ages

Decent work and economic growth

Promote sustained, inclusive & sustainable economic growth, full & productive employment and decent work for all

Responsible consumption & production

Ensure sustainable consumption & production patterns

Gender equality

Achieve gender equality & empower all women & girls

Reduced inequalities

Reduce inequality within & among countries

Our ESG Commitments

Our key pillars of impact tie up with the overall ESG commitments of promoting economic empowerment, equity, social security, safety, and a better quality of life for all our stakeholders as we organise home and beauty services.

Social

  • Middle-class earnings: Committed to service partners earning at least a living wage & leading a middle-class life
  • Safety net: Provide life, accidental & health insurance cover to all partners
  • Train & upskill 500,000 service partners by 2030, including at least 200,000 women service partners
  • Partner Stock Option Plan: Create an evergreen PSOP plan, enabling 20,000-30,000 partners to become shareholders of UC

Governance

  • Independent and diverse board, with a one share one vote philosophy
  • Increase women representation in leadership roles in the company to 40% by 2025
  • Train & upskill 500,000 service partners by 2030, including at least 200,000 women service partners
  • Create a diverse and inclusive workplace, helping people of all backgrounds unlock their potential

Environment

  • Encourage EV adoption and minimize single-use plastic from service delivery and supply chain
  • Encourage reuse & recycle of home appliances and build a supply chain of safe e-waste disposal
  • Build an ethical supply chain and define clean sourcing norms

Partner Enablement

We have always believed in a structured approach towards partner development and well-being centered around 4 key pillars: improved earnings, safety net, training, and wealth creation.
